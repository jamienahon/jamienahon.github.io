Portfolio

Blight

Blight is a 3D action souls-like that was made as part of the second year major
production assessment at AIE. The game centres on an ex-hunter who sets out to
destroy an Eldritch General who is corrupting the isles of Yorlock from another
realm. With the help of the Hunter's trusty bow and arrow, and some perseverence,
the Hunter hopes to destroy this Eldritch Deity for good.

This game took 6 months and 11 team members to complete.

Itch.io Page: blindmunchgames.itch.io/blight
Github Page: github.com/jamienahon/Blight

Rainward

Rainward is a 3D action-platformer that was made as part of the first year major
production assessment at AIE. The game places you in control of a wombat named
Thorn with the aim of defeating the cruel goblins attempting to de-forest Thorn's home.
To achieve this goal, the player must jump, collect, and fight their way to victory.

This game took 6 weeks and 11 team members to complete.

Itch.io Page: tristan-thorpe.itch.io/rainward
Github Page: github.com/jamienahon/Rainward

Galactic Grill

Galactic Grill is a VR work-simulation game that was made as part of
the second year Virtual and Extended Realities unit at AIE. The game
puts the player in charge of a fast food resturant where they will have
to serve alien customers burgers with wierd alien ingredients.

This game took 4 weeks and 9 team members to complete.

Itch.io Page: brodie-frazier.itch.io/galactic-grill
Github Page: github.com/jamienahon/Galactic-Grill

Fungi Frenzy

Fungi Frenzy is an endless runner that was made as a submission for the
Mini Jam: 156: Mushroom Game Jam. The game tasks you with running through a forest
filled with obstacles for as long as you can to try and accumulate the highest score
possible. During each run you will come across several items that will help in your
journey through the dangerous forest.

This game took 72 hours and 3 team members to complete.

Itch.io Page: jimseyboy258.itch.io/fungi-frenzy
Github Page: github.com/jamienahon/Fungi-Frenzy
